Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025

1. Overview

This service (“Call/SMS Directory”) allows you to call or text a single phone number to reach a menu of predefined contacts. Voice calls are connected to the contact you choose; SMS messages are relayed between you and the chosen contact.

2. Information We Collect and Use

We use your phone number only to:

  • Verify that you are allowed to use the service (allowlist).
  • Connect your calls and relay your messages to the contact you select.
  • Operate and improve the service (e.g., call and message logs for troubleshooting).

3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not share, sell, or rent your mobile phone number or other personal information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We may share data only as needed with service providers (e.g., telephony/SMS providers) to deliver the service, or when required by law.

4. Data Retention and Security

Call and message metadata may be stored for as long as needed to operate the service and for legitimate business or legal purposes. We take reasonable steps to protect your information.

5. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your data by contacting the operator of this service. You can opt out of SMS at any time by replying STOP to messages from this service.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top will be revised when changes are made.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the administrator of this Call/SMS Directory service.
